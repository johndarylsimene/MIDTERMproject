ABOUT US

In this world full of pretending to be someone else, Jyrald was inspired to create Nemesis to make everyone standout on what they already have. It's not about who has the blemish-free skin, the perfect eyeshadow, the on-fleek brows, or who had the most pigmented lipstick. Here on Nemesis we want you to love who you are and that our products are only here to accentuate you and your features.

Nemesis are designed to cater to wide variety of skin tones all-around the globe. And as we release quality products, we also make sure its affordable for you to enjoy it. We also listen for your opinions, so message us on our social networking sites and we will reply to you.
